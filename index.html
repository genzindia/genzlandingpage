About Us

Teenage years can be challenging as our bodies and minds are exposed to new realities every day. Managing emotions and dealing with limitations can be overwhelming. While these struggles are normal, the fast-paced world doesn't always give teenagers enough time to prepare for the competitive and demanding real world.

Hello, I am Naina, a seasoned teacher and counselor with over 5 years of experience. Throughout my career, I have guided over 100 kids in various areas, including their studies, life struggles, confidence issues, and career choices.

In my journey working with kids, I have realized that active family support and proper guidance play a crucial role in shaping their overall personality. That's why our course has been designed to cover all dimensions of a teenager's personality. From everyday struggles with people, poor time management, difficulties in expressing thoughts, to being overly focused on virtual screens like Instagram and Facebook, our course addresses it all.

We believe that personality development is a skill that can be acquired, not something that is inherited. With this belief, we have put together our best efforts to create a personality development camp specifically for teenagers - the leaders of tomorrow! We are committed to providing the right guidance and support to help teenagers become well-rounded individuals who are equipped to thrive in the real world.
